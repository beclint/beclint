## YouTube Description

In the ancient city-states of Hausaland, a chief's daughter named Binta made a promise that would change her life forever. She agreed to marry a serpent — and on the night of the full moon, he came for her.

This is the Hausa folktale of the girl who kept her word, the prince cursed to live as a snake, and the fire that destroyed everything.

🏛️ **Origin:** Hausa people, Northern Nigeria (West Africa)
📖 **Moral:** A promise is stronger than strength — "Wanda ya ci amana, ba shi da gari" (The one who breaks trust has no home)
