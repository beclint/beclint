## YouTube Description

An ancient crocodile rose from the depths of Lake Natron and offered two brothers a choice: complete a simple task and earn the secret of the lake. One brother ran. One brother walked. Only one understood the lesson.

This is the Maasai folktale of the twins who learned that patience is the mother of all virtues.

🏛️ **Origin:** Maasai people, Kenya/Tanzania (East Africa)
📖 **Moral:** Patience is the mother of all virtues — "Emoniata oo nkituak"
